Getting Started

Welcome to the DCF community, thanks for joining!

To get started, we need to get you ready for your potential counsellor. To do that, you will need to provide us with more information about you, and your finances. We will then take a look at what you have provided us and find you the most suitable counsellor.

Of course, you will be able to find your own counsellor, simply search for them by their name and get into a chat with them about your counselling.
